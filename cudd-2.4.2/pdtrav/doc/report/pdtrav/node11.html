TR management

Functions for creating and manipulating transition relations are provided by this package. More specifically:

  • TR creation: from next-state functions and variable arrays (inputs, present/next-state). From scratch (GpCTo be done.).
  • TR sorting: variants of the IWLS95 heuristics are provided, the goal being a good partition sorting for efficient early smoothing with conjunctive partitioning
  • TR clustering: linear conjunction of partitions controlled by a size threshold.
  • TR subsetting: an external constraint is used to constrain a TR by means of conjunction.
  • TR partitioning
  • Abstractions oriented to approximate reachability.
